THE STORY OF THE
Narangala Retreat Cabin

BUILD IN 2022

Discover Narangala Retreat Cabin, your serene escape nestled among the captivating Narangala mountains. With rustic charm and modern comforts, soak in panoramic views from the spacious deck and reconnect with nature's tranquility.

Perfect for couples and small groups seeking solace and unforgettable moments in the heart of Narangala's breathtaking beauty.
